Hello New ÜberBots,

This Tuesday, September 17th, is our first meeting of the year with new members. we are calling it our "New Recruits Day." At this meeting, we will have a small introduction about the team, some time to drive robots, food, drinks, and some time to meet the current members of the team. This meeting will take place from 7 PM - 9 PM in the Avon High School Community Room. We will also have robots and members out in the Gallery. We recommend to attend regardless of whether or not you are sure about joining the team. It will be a fun time and a nice opportunity to meet different people of different grades within the school.

The Avon High School Robotics Team is a part of a program called FIRST, this stands for "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology." The ÜberBots face off against many different teams from across New England in different competitions. Our next competition, Bash @ the Beach, will be on October 5th.

In addition to competition, the team works towards spreading the ideals of science, technology, and business through the use of various Community outreach events. An example of this is coming up this Saturday, September 21st. The ÜberBots has a booth and stage time for Avon Day.

Some of you may be unsure as to whether you will be able to contribute to the team. However, all talents are needed in order to continue this successful program. For the past few years, we have even used the slogan "There's a Place for you Here," because all talents can be used on this team. Interests in photography, journalism, engineering, community outreach, gaming, math, science, art, English, mechanics, accounting, public speaking, project management, writing, and so much more are all needed for the ÜberBots.

Please come this Tuesday if you are available. For more information on the team, please visit http://www.uberbots.org/. For more information on FIRST Robotics, please visithttp://www.usfirst.org/. Email us with anything at management@uberbots.org.

Thanks,
The Management Team
